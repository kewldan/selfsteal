Analytics your whole team will actually use

From the first event to your board deck, Metricly gives product, growth and engineering a shared source of truth — self-serve, real time, and beautifully fast.

Live product metrics

Track DAUs, activation, engagement and revenue in dashboards that update the moment an event lands — no overnight jobs, no stale numbers.

Funnels & drop-off

Build multi-step funnels in a click and see exactly where users stall. Break any step down by plan, device, or cohort in seconds.

Retention cohorts

Understand which features bring users back. Weekly and monthly retention curves reveal your true product-market fit signal.

Behavioral segments

Define audiences from any combination of events and traits, then sync them to your CRM, ads and lifecycle tools automatically.

Anomaly alerts

Metricly watches every metric and pings Slack the moment something spikes or drops — so you hear about issues before your users do.

Governed & secure

SOC 2 Type II, SSO/SAML, granular roles and a tracking plan that keeps every event clean, documented and trustworthy.

How it works

From install to insight in an afternoon

No warehouse migration, no six-week onboarding. Drop in the SDK, send your events, and start answering questions the same day.

Connect your data

Install the lightweight SDK for web, iOS, Android or server — or stream straight from your warehouse. One line to autocapture core events.

Model & explore

Define the metrics that matter, group events into a tidy tracking plan, and slice everything by cohort, plan or property without SQL.

Act on it

Share live dashboards, set anomaly alerts, and push segments to the tools your team already uses. Insight that actually reaches decisions.
