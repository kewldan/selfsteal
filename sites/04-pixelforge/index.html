About the Studio

A tiny team with an oversized imagination

Pixelforge is an independent studio of 15 developers, artists, and musicians working out of a converted print house in Malmö, Sweden. We started in 2019 with a single arcade prototype and a rule we still keep: if it doesn't feel great in the first thirty seconds, we rebuild it.

We are fully self-funded, ship on our own timeline, and answer only to the players who wishlist and follow our work. No live-service treadmills, no predatory monetization — just finished games we are proud to put our name on.
