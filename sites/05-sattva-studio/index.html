Your guides

Meet the teachers

A small, devoted team who teach from years of their own practice — steady, warm and endlessly patient.

Maya Ellison

Founder & Lead Vinyasa

Maya opened Sattva after a decade of teaching across Portland and Rishikesh. Her flows are musical, grounded and quietly demanding.

RYT-500 · Yoga Medicine® trained

Devi Ramachandran

Yin, Prenatal & Restorative

Devi holds space like few others — slow, precise and deeply kind. A doula and prenatal specialist, she makes every student feel held.

RPYT · Yin certified · Doula

Jonah Brooks

Power Vinyasa & Breathwork

Former endurance athlete turned breath nerd. Jonah's classes are strong and playful, with an emphasis on the breath beneath the effort.

RYT-200 · Pranayama & mobility
