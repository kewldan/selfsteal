Our Story

A corner kitchen with an open heart.

The Copper Fork began in 2016 as a twelve-seat supper club in a converted ironmonger's shop on Larkspur Row. Word travelled, the walls came down, and the bistro you'll find today grew up around that same worn copper pass.

Chef Marguerite Vance cooks the way she was raised to — nothing wasted, everything tasted twice, and the best ingredient always wins. She keeps the menu short on purpose so the kitchen can chase what's ripe that week.

"We're not trying to reinvent dinner. We just want it to taste like someone who loves you made it."

Reservations

Save your seat at the pass.

We hold a handful of tables for walk-ins each night, but the dining room fills quickly — especially on weekends. Booking ahead is the surest way in.

  • Parties of 1–6 can be booked here. For larger groups or private buy-outs, call us at (415) 555-0192.
  • Seatings run every 30 minutes from 5:00 PM. We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes.
  • Tell us about allergies or celebrations in the notes — we love to make a night feel like yours.

By requesting a table you agree to our 24-hour cancellation courtesy. We'll confirm by email within one business day.
